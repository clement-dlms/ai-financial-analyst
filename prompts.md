# Claude Prompts

> [!note]
> Thes prompts will help oriente your AI and stay precise on the AI's responses.

## Templates

### Prompt 1

# Objective

I want to analyze [COMPANY] as a long-term investor.

Use the appropriate equity research skills.

# Investment Horizon

My timeframe is:

[3 years / 5 years / 10 years]

# Perspective

Analyze from the perspective of:

[Growth investor / Value investor / Quality investor / Risk-focused investor]

# Analysis Required

Perform:

- Financial Analysis
- Competitive Analysis
- Moat Analysis
- Management Analysis
- Industry Analysis
- Valuation Analysis
- Risk Analysis

# Key Questions

Answer:

1. What makes this company exceptional or average?

2. What assumptions are embedded in the current valuation?

3. What could make the thesis succeed?

4. What could permanently damage the business?

5. What does the market possibly misunderstand?

# Required Output

Provide:

- Executive summary
- Evidence-based analysis
- Bull case
- Base case
- Bear case
- Key risks
- Important assumptions
- Questions I should continue monitoring

Separate:

Facts

Assumptions

Analyst interpretation
